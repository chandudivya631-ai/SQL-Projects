# Retail Sales Analysis

## Project Objective
Analyze retail sales data to identify sales trends, top-performing products, customer spending patterns, and category-wise revenue.

## Business Questions
1. What is the total revenue?
2. Which products generate the highest revenue?
3. What are the monthly sales trends?
4. Who are the top customers?
5. Which product categories perform the best?

## Tools Used
- SQL
- MySQL
- Excel
- Power BI

## Key Insights
- Identified top-selling products.
- Analyzed monthly sales performance.
- Evaluated customer purchasing behavior.
- Compared revenue across product categories.
